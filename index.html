Have a question? I may have the answer!

A little bit of everything! Questions to know more about me or questions I hear daily at every family get together.

When will you be graduating from SDSU?

I'm expecting to graduate after this semester for class of Spring 2024. But, I've discovered something confusing in my degree audit and now I'm crying and scared.

What do you have planned for after graduation?

I plan to work at the my old restaurant job as a hostess to save money while developing my portfolio. To save up money, while looking for worthwhile job openings I'd actually want to pursue. Rather than applying for everything right away. Take my time with it!

What was your favorite class you took at SDSU?

My favorite class had to be Graphic Design IV. It was the smallest class turnout I ever had with only 6 students including myself. But the work and interaction with a small enviornment was super fun. I also discovered that I wanted to pursue cafe and restauran branding!

What work are you most proud producing at SDSU?

Probably my cafe rebrand guidelines for a cafe in convoy! The space and brand has so much potential and I had a lot of fun designing it!

What are your favorite colors?

I love the color yellow! I think I'm a pretty upbeat and sunshiny type of demeanor so I love having the color yellow around to brighten the environment I'm in! I don't like wearing the color yellow though, it doesn't compliment me very well.

What’s on your bucket list?

I know for sure I want to travel. It's intimidating thinking of where to though. I've been to Japan, but I want to go back already. That'd far as where I am so far.
